Spawns & Signals

Right-click a track tile to spawn a train along it, or to add a block signal facing either way. A signal protects the block ahead — the connected track up to the next signal or a dead end — with an automatic distant marker on the tile before it (green = clear, orange = taken). Mains stay green while their block is clear; a train waits at a red main and pulls away shortly after it clears. A signal direction is disabled when there is no clean track to host its distant.
